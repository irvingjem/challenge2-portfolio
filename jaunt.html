About

Project:

Branding for a modern day disposible camera. A new Logo, colors systems, advertising posters, and typography was created. With Jaunt we have made a new product experience of purchasing a disposable camera for all experience levels. The camera comes with its own safe box making it a product you can easily take anywhere. Inside you will find a document with a few tips of tips and tricks that are easily achiveable with disposable cameras. The list also contains different suggestions of places that you can take or send your film to develop it, either by a drugstore or professionally done.
