Jem Irving

Jem Irving is a Designer in Portland, OR. Graduated from PNCA (Pacific Northwest College of Art). A little about Jem, he has dual citizenship (AUS)-(US). In his current practice he has a passion for creating well designed UI/UX creating enjoyable experiences and fun daily work for his instagram. Having Traveled a lot in his youth, he was inspired by all the places he was able to visit and all the different styles of design.
